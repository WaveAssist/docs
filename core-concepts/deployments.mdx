---
title: "Deployments"
description: "Learn about managing deployments in WaveAssist."
---

**Deployments** allow you to run your AI assistants and nodes in a live environment. You can start, stop, and monitor deployments directly from the dashboard.

### Key Features

- **Start Deployments**: Launch your AI assistants.
- **Stop Deployments**: Halt running AI assistants.
- **Monitor Deployments**: View logs and status of deployments.
