---
title: "Deployments"
description: "Learn about managing deployments in WaveAssist."
---

**Deployments** allow you to run your workflows and nodes in a live environment. You can start, stop, and monitor deployments directly from the dashboard.

### Key Features
- **Start Deployments**: Launch your workflows.
- **Stop Deployments**: Halt running workflows.
- **Monitor Deployments**: View logs and status of deployments.
